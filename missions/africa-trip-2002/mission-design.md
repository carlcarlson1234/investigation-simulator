# Mission: The Africa Trip (September 2002)

## Mission Overview

**Title:** The Africa Trip
**Subtitle:** September 2002 · Clinton, Epstein, Spacey
**Duration:** 9 days (September 21-30, 2002)
**Difficulty:** Introductory — this is the first mission, designed to teach investigation mechanics

**Key Question:** *A former president, a convicted sex trafficker, a disgraced actor, and four unidentified young women flew across Africa together. What was really happening?*

---

## Narrative Arc (3 Acts)

### ACT 1: "The Invitation" (Intro + Context Cards)

The player opens a sealed case file. Before the evidence pack, they see **context cards** — news clippings and background info that frame the investigation:

**Context Card 1 — The Trip Announcement**
> In September 2002, former President Bill Clinton embarked on a 9-day humanitarian trip across Africa, visiting HIV/AIDS project sites in five countries. He flew on a private Boeing 727 — owned by financier Jeffrey Epstein.

URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Relationship_of_Bill_Clinton_and_Jeffrey_Epstein

**Context Card 2 — The Passenger List**
> The plane carried an unusual mix: actors Kevin Spacey and Chris Tucker, Epstein's companion Ghislaine Maxwell, Clinton aide Doug Band, LA entertainment mogul Casey Wasserman, billionaire Ron Burkle, and several unidentified young women described as "a masseuse, a model, and a ballerina."

URL: https://www.newsweek.com/kevin-spacey-admits-flying-jeffery-epstien-bill-clinton-1911631

**Context Card 3 — The Secret Service Question**
> Clinton claimed Secret Service agents accompanied him on all legs. But the flight manifests show no Secret Service agents listed — and the Secret Service refused to confirm their presence on the Africa or Asia trips.

URL: https://www.washingtonexaminer.com/news/2032841/flight-manifests-reveal-bill-clinton-traveled-with-epstein-six-times-not-the-four-times-he-admitted/

**Context Card 4 — Years Later**
> In 2024, Kevin Spacey admitted there were "young girls" on the flight. He said he had "no idea who Epstein was" when he boarded. Clinton's office maintains he "knows nothing about the terrible crimes."

URL: https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/kevin-spacey-admits-flying-jeffrey-133301996.html

## Discovery Beats (Key "AHA" Moments)

These are the moments the mission is designed to deliver:

1. **"Ghislaine organized this."** Reading the Sept 10 email and realizing Slade sent the trip logistics TO Ghislaine, not FROM her. She was the contact point for Clinton's team.

2. **"Dan Rather wanted on the plane."** CBS News wanted to put Dan Rather + 3 crew on Epstein's private jet. The intersection of media, politics, and Epstein's world.

3. **"No Secret Service."** The manifests don't list any agents. Clinton claimed they were always there. The Secret Service wouldn't confirm.

4. **"Young women nobody can name."** The FBI physician's notes describe 4 women aged 20-22 — a masseuse, a model, a ballerina. Names redacted. Chauntae Davies was one.

5. **"Casey Wasserman's emails."** After the trip, Wasserman's emails to Maxwell turned intimate. He's now running the 2028 LA Olympics. The trip was the introduction.

6. **"The matching shirts."** Clinton and Epstein wearing the same patterned shirts, smiling together. The level of comfort and closeness.

7. **"The Clinton painting."** Not from the trip, but found at Epstein's property: Clinton in a blue dress. What does it mean about their relationship?

8. **"It kept happening."** The 2009 "Breakdown of Costs for Africa Trip" emails — seven years later, still planning Africa trips together.
